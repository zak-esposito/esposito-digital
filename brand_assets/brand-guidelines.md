# Esposito Digital — Brand Guidelines

## Brand Name
Esposito Digital

## Tagline Options (pick one or let Claude suggest)
- "Websites. Apps. Automations."
- "Digital solutions that actually work."
- "Built for businesses that move fast."

## Colour Palette
- Primary Background: #0A0A0A (near-black)
- Secondary Background: #111111 (dark grey, for card surfaces)
- Elevated Surface: #1A1A1A (slightly lighter, for hover states)
- Accent: #3B82F6 (electric blue — confident, techy, not generic)
- Accent Hover: #2563EB (darker blue)
- Text Primary: #F5F5F5 (off-white, softer than pure white)
- Text Secondary: #9CA3AF (muted grey for body/subtitles)
- Accent Glow: rgba(59, 130, 246, 0.15) (for subtle glow effects)

## Typography
- Headings: A bold, modern display font (NOT Inter, NOT Roboto — something with character. Suggestions: "Outfit", "Satoshi", "Cabinet Grotesk", "Plus Jakarta Sans")
- Body: A clean readable sans-serif that pairs well with the heading font
- Heading weight: 700-800
- Body weight: 400
- Large headings: tight letter-spacing (-0.03em)
- Body text: generous line-height (1.7)

## Tone
- Professional but not corporate
- Confident but not arrogant
- Technical but accessible
- Speaks to small/medium business owners, not developers

## Logo
- No logo yet — use the text "ESPOSITO DIGITAL" as a wordmark in the heading font
- Consider a subtle monogram "ED" as a favicon

## Services
1. **Websites** — Fast, professional websites for local businesses. Mobile-first, SEO-ready, deployed and live in days not months.
2. **Web Applications** — Custom web apps, dashboards, and tools built to solve specific business problems.
3. **AI Workflows & Automations** — Intelligent automations that save time and money. From lead follow-up to data processing.

## Target Audience
- Small to medium local businesses (Wales/UK)
- Business owners who know they need a web presence but don't know where to start
- People who've been quoted £2k+ by agencies and want something more accessible

## Site Sections (single page)
1. Hero — Name, tagline, brief value prop, CTA button
2. Services — The three services above with icons or subtle visuals
3. About — Brief section about Zak / the business (keep it human, not corporate)
4. Portfolio — Placeholder section for future work (can say "coming soon" or show mockups)
5. Contact — Simple contact section with email and/or a contact form
6. Footer — Links, socials, copyright
